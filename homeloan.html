Immediate loan approval

All our home loans are instantly approved with low interest rates for CFS account holders.

Home loans for your housing needs

CFS offers low interest home loans with competitive rates. Our home loans — and low home loan rates — are designed to meet your house financing needs.

We offer conventional fixed-rate loans, ARMs, mortgage, FHA loans, VA loans and jumbo loans. We also offer housing loans for new construction and investment property. Our home loan options allow you to pay in installments of ten years. With our easy-to-use calculator, you can estimate your monthly payments, find out how much house you can afford, and more.

Our home loan schemes

  • Fixed-rate home loan - A home loan for homeowners who are looking for predictability.
  • Adjustable-rate mortgage home loan - A home loan for home buyers with lower credit scores.
  • FHA home loan - A home loan for home buyers with meager savings for a down payment.
  • VA home loan - A home loan for veterans who’ve served 90 days or more.
  • USDA home loan - A home loan for families in rural areas who are struggling financially.
  • Bridge home loan - A home loan for homeowners with excellent credit and a low debt-to-income ratio.

Home loan requirements

  • Home loan amount – FHA home loans have maximum mortgage limits that vary by state and county.
  • Home loan down Payment – FHA home loan guidelines require a minimum down payment of 3.5 percent.
  • Home loan property condition – FHA home loans require that the home being purchased must meet certain conditions.
