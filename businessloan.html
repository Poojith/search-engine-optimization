Business loans

If you're a business owner or an enterprise, a CFS business loan is the best loan option for you. Our business loans provide a specific amount of credit to finance major expenses, such as the expansion of operating facilities or the purchase of new or used equipment, including vehicles. Regardless of the business loan amount, you’ll enjoy competitive loan rates. Business loans can be unsecured, or secured by collateral. If you’re making an investment in your business, a business loan can help you cover long-term financing.

Loans can be unsecured, or secured by collateral. With our easy payment processing, you can enable automatic deduction from your CFS checking account and utilize full amortization up to seven years.

With Startup business loan plus, startup businesses can borrow best business loans to enhance business outcomes. Need help with business loans? Use our business loan planner to help you get started with business loan ideas.
