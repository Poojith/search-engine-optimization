What is CFS eChecking?

eChecking is a basic checking account that comes with standard benefits like a debit card that doubles as an ATM card. It also gives you full access to Online and Mobile Banking,a suite of powerful tools and apps that make it easy to track your spending, pay bills, send money, deposit checks and more.

  • Online banking Service

    With CFS eBanking you can check your balance and account activity or search your statements and payment history whenever you like from your laptop, smartphone or any mobile devices. Bank on your schedule with Mobile Banking and get the convenience and security of Online Banking from almost anywhere.And it is totally FREE!

  • No Service Fee

    A totally free checking account with online banking service at CFS.Students with no or low income are welcome to open a checking account with us. No additional fee for opening checking account online with minimum $100 deposite.
