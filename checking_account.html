What does CFS Checking Account offers?

CFS eChecking is a basic checking account that comes with standard benefits like a debit card that doubles as an ATM card and eBanking service. It also gives you full access to Online banking and Mobile Banking,a suite of powerful tools and apps that make it easy to track your spending, pay bills, send money, deposit checks and more.

  • Free Online banking checking account

    CFS eBanking service is accessible through all of our products including our checking account. We offer free online checking accounts and online banking solutions.Use your online checking account for fast, easy, convenient online bill payment. You can automatically process payments online, as well as schedule and track individual, future and recurring payments.With CFS eBanking you can check your balance and account activity or search your statements and payment history whenever you like from your laptop, smartphone or any mobile devices. Bank on your schedule with Mobile Banking and get the convenience and security of Online Banking from almost anywhere.And it is totally FREE!

  • No Service Fee checking account

    CFS Checking Account is a totally free bank account with online banking service at CFS.Students with no or low income are welcome to open a checking account with us. No additional fee for opening checking account online with minimum $100 deposite.

  • Easy to apply Checking Account Online

    CFS makes it easy to apply free bank account online. Apply through filling out this Application form and email to us with a minimum deposit $100.
